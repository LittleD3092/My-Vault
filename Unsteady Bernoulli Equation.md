# Equation

SImilar to [[Bernoulli's Equations]], we start from [[Euler's Equation]]:

$$
\rho\frac{D\vec V}{Dt} = 
\rho \vec g - \nabla p
$$

$$\implies \frac{D \vec V}{Dt} = -\frac 1 \rho \nabla p - g \hat k$$
