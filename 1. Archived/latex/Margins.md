可以一次設定四邊

```latex
\usepackage[margin = marginsize]{geometry}
```

```latex
\usepackage[top = topmargin,
			bottom = bottommargin,
			left = leftmargin,
			right = rightmargin]{geometry}
```

`marginsize`, `topmargin`, `bottommargin`, `leftmargin`, `rightmargin` 填入想要的值
