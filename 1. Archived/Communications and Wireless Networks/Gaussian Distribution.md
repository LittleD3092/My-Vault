The Gaussian distribution $\mathcal{N}(\mu, \sigma^2)$ has the following formula: (<u>1</u>)

$$f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2\pi}} 
e^{
	\displaystyle
	-\frac{1}{2}
	\left(
		\frac{x - \mu}{\sigma}
	\right)^2
}$$
