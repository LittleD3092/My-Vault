Multiple-access (random access) means that the channel is not divided, and transmissions from multiple transmitters may collide.

There are some random access schems: (<u>1</u>)

- [[ALOHA]]
- [[Slotted ALOHA]]
- [[CSMA]]
	- [[Exponential Random Backoff]]
- [[CSMA-CA]]
- [[CSMA-CD]]
