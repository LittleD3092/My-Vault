An alpha particle is emitted when [[Alpha Decay|alpha decay]] happens. (<u>1</u>)

An alpha particle is identical to the nucleus of a helium-4 atom, which is consisted of 2 protons and 2 neutrons.

$${}^4_2\alpha^{2+}$$
