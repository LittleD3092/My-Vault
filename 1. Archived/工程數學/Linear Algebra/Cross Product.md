掐頭去尾插三下

$$\vec{ a } \times \vec{ b } = \vec{ c }$$

$$
\implies
\bcancel{ \cancel{ \begin{array}{}
	a_1 \\
	b_1
\end{array} } }
\begin{array}{}
	a_2 & a_3 & a_1 & a_2 \\
	b_2 & b_3 & b_1 & b_2
\end{array}
\bcancel{ \cancel{ 
\begin{array}{}
	a_3 \\
	b_3
\end{array}
} }
$$

$$
\implies
\begin{array}{}
	a_2 && a_3 && a_1 && a_2 \\
	& \times && \times && \times \\
	b_2 && b_3 && b_1 && b_2
\end{array}
$$

$$
\implies
c_1 = 
\left\vert
	\begin{array}{}
		a_2 & a_3 \\
		b_2 & b_3
	\end{array}
\right\vert
\quad 
c_2 = 
\left\vert
	\begin{array}{}
		a_3 & a_1 \\
		b_3 & b_1
	\end{array}
\right\vert
\quad 
c_3 = 
\left\vert
	\begin{array}{}
		a_1 & a_2 \\
		b_1 & b_2
	\end{array}
\right\vert
$$
