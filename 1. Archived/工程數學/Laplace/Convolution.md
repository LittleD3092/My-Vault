中文叫做旋積

$$f(t) * g(t) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(\tau)g(t - \tau)d\tau$$
$$*: \;\text{convolution}$$
.
$$\text{When}\; f(t) = 0 \;\text{for}\; t < 0 \;\text{and}\; g(t) = 0 \;\text{for}\; t < 0$$
$$\text{上方式子可簡化為下方}$$
$$f(t)*g(t) = \int_0^tf(\tau)g(t - \tau)d\tau$$
