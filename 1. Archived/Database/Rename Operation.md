Rename operation is used to rename the columns and the table. It can be written as:

$$\rho_x(E)$$

- $E$: Expression.
- $x$: The list of name in the form of $x(A_1, A_2, \dots, A_n)$.
	- $x$: New name of table.
	- $A_1, \dots, A_n$: New attributes name.
