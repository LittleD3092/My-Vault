The expectation of a random variable $X$ is given by:

$$
\begin{array}{l}
	E[X] &=& \displaystyle \sum_x xp(x) \\
	E[X] &=& \displaystyle \int xp(x) dx
\end{array}$$

It has the property:

$$E[aX+b] = aE[X] + b$$

- $a, b$: Arbitrary numerical values.
