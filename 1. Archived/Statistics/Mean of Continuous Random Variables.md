The mean of a continuous random variable can be calculated using the [[Probability Density Function|PDF]]:

$$\mu = E(X) = \int_{-\infty}^\infty xf(x)dx$$

- $\mu$: Mean.
- $E(X)$: Expected value of $X$.
- $f(x)$: [[Probability Density Function|PDF]].
