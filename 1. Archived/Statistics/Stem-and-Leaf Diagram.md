In stem-and-leaf diagram, each observation (number) is divided into stems and leaves. (<u>1</u>)

For example:

$$
\begin{array}{}
	\text{data: } 21, 24, 24, 26, 27, 27, 30, 32, 38, 41 \\
	\left.
		\begin{array}{r}
			\text{Stems} \\
			2 \\
			3 \\
			4
		\end{array} \,
	\right\vert
	\left. \,
		\begin{array}{l}
			\text{Leaves} \\
			144677 \\
			028 \\
			1
		\end{array}
	\right.
\end{array}
$$
