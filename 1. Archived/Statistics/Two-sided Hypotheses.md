Two-sided hypotheses has one hypothesis of a specific value, and one hypothesis of not a specific value. (<u>1</u>)

For example, whether or not the mean burning rate is $50\ \text{cm/s}$:

$$
\begin{array}{}
	H_0: & \mu &=& 50\text{ cm/s} \\
	H_1: & \mu &\neq& 50 \text{ cm/s}
\end{array}
$$
