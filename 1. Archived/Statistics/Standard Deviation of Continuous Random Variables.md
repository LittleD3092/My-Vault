The standard deviation can be calculated using [[Variance of Continuous Random Variables|variance]]. (<u>1</u>)

$$\sigma^2 = V(X)$$

- $\sigma$: Standard deviation.
- $V(X)$: [[Variance of Continuous Random Variables|Variance]].
