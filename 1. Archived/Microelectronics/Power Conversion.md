Microelectronics focuses on the circuit handling power conversion. There are 4 types: (<u>1</u>)

- DC to DC ([[DC Chopper]])
- DC to AC ([[Inverter]])
- AC to AC ([[Rectifier]])
- AC to AC ([[Transformer]], [[Frequency Converter]], [[Matrix Converter]])
