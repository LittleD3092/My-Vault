Heat flux can be defined by [[Fourier's Law]]:

$$q_x'' = -k\frac{dT}{dx}$$

- $q_x''$: Heat flux. ${\rm W}/{\rm m^2}$.
- $dT/dx$: Temperature gradient.
- $k$: Thermal conductivity. ${\rm W/m \cdot K}$.
