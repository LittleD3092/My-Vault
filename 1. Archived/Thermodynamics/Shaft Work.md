$$\delta W = F\cdot dx$$
$$ = F\cdot r d\theta$$
.
$$\dot{ W } = \frac{ \delta W }{ dt } = \frac{ F\cdot dx }{ dt } = F \cdot v$$
$v$: velocity

$$ = \frac{ Frd\theta }{ dt } = T \cdot \omega$$
$T$: torque
$\omega$: angular velocity
