$$
\left\{
	\begin{array}{}
		F = ma \\
		a = \frac{ dv }{ dt }
	\end{array}
\right.
$$

$$
\left\{
	\begin{array}{}
		F = m\frac{ dV }{ dt } \\
		v = \frac{ ds }{ dt } \\
		ds = vdt
	\end{array}
\right.
\quad
\text{ 代入下面的式子 }
$$

$$W_{ acceleration } = \int_1^2 F\cdot ds$$

$$ = \int_1^2 (m \cdot \frac{ dv }{ dt }) \cdot (vdt)$$

$$ = \int_1^2 mvdv$$

$$ = \frac{ 1 }{ 2 }m(v_2^2 - v_1^2)$$
