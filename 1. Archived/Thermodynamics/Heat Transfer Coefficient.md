# Natural Convection

Natural Convection 意思是自然對流，沒有外力作用

- gas: $5 \sim 25$
- liquid: $50 \sim 1000$

# Forced Convection

強制對流的情況

- gas: $25 \sim 250$
- liquid: $50 \sim 20000$

# Boiling Phase Change

$2500 \sim 100000$
