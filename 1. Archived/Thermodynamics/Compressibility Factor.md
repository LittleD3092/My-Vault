因為氣體通常都不是[[Ideal Gas|理想氣體]]，所以用一個常數 $z$ 修正

$$z = \frac{ P \nu }{ RT } \qquad z = 1 \text{ for ideal gas }$$

# 特性

1. At all temperatures $z \rightarrow 1$ as $P \rightarrow 0$
2. At $T = 300K$ and above $z \approx 1$
