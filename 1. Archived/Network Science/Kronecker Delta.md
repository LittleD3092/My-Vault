Kronecker delta is a function to see whether two objects are the same. (<u>1</u>)

$$\delta_{g_ig_j} = \left\{
	\begin{array}{}
		0, & \text{if } g_i \neq g_j \\
		1, & \text{if } g_i = g_j
	\end{array}
\right.$$
