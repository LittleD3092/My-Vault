The total number of $r$ hop walk from $i$ to $j$ is denoted by

$$N_{ij}^{(r)} = \left[
	{\bf A}^r
\right]_{ij}$$

- $[{\bf A}^r]_{ij}$: The $ij$th element of matrix $\bf A$ multiplies itself by $r$ times.
