The sample standard deviation $s$ is the square root of the [[Sample Variance|sample variance]]. (<u>1</u>)

$$s = \sqrt{
	\frac{
		\displaystyle
		\sum_{i = 1}^n (x_i - \bar x)^2
	}{n - 1}
}$$
