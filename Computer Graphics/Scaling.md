The [[Transformation Matrix]] for scaling is as follows:

$$
{\bf S} = {\bf S}(s_x, s_y, s_z) = 
\left[
	\begin{array}{}
		s_x & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
		0 & s_y & 0 & 0 \\
		0 & 0 & s_z & 0 \\
		0 & 0 & 0 & 1
	\end{array}
\right]
$$
