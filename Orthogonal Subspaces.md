Two subspaces $X$ and $Y$ are said to be **orthogonal** if ${\bf x}^T{\bf y} = 0$ for every ${\bf x} \in X$ and every ${\bf y}\in Y$. If $X$ and $Y$ are orthogonal, we write $X\perp Y$.
