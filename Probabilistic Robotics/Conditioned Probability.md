The distribution of $X = x$ given that $Y = y$ can be represented as:

$$p(x \vert y) = p(X = x\vert Y = y)$$

It can also be written using [[Joint Distribution|joint distribution]]:

$$p(x\vert y) = \frac{p(x, y)}{p(y)}$$
