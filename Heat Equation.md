For 1D problem, heat flow ($v$) from high to low temperature ($u$) in proportion to the temperature gradient.

$$v = -K\frac{du}{dx}$$

For 3D, we use vector form

$$\vec v = -K\left[
	\begin{array}{}
		\partial u / \partial x \\
		\partial u / \partial y \\
		\partial u / \partial z
	\end{array}
\right] = -K\text{grad} (u)$$

$u = u(x, y, z, t)$

- thermal conductivity $K$
- specific heat $\sigma$
