A fuel cell consists of: (<u>2</u>)

- Fuel (often hydrogen)
- Oxidizing agent (often oxygen)

Fuel cell converts chemical energy of a fuel to electricity, as the following figure shows: (<u>1</u>, <u>2</u>)
