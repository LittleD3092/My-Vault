$$\tau = G\gamma$$

$\tau$: [[Shear Stress]]
$G$: Modulus of Rigidity(Shear Modulus)
$\gamma$: [[Shear Strain]]

# $\tau_{\text{MAX}}$

$$\tau_{\text{MAX}} = G\gamma_{\text{MAX}}$$
$$ = \underline{Gr\theta}_{\#}$$

$$\underline{\tau = \frac{\rho}{r}\tau_{\text{MAX}}}_{\#}$$
