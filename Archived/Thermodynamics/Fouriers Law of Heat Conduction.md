$$Q = -kA\frac{ dT }{ dx }$$
> $k$: [[Thermal Conductivity]]
> - $\sim 100 W/m\cdot K$ for metal
> $K$: Kelvin
> - $1 \sim 10 W/m\cdot K$ non-metal solid
> - $0.1 \sim 10 W/m\cdot K$ for liquid
> - $0.1 \downarrow W/m\cdot K$ for gas
> 
> $A$: 熱傳面積
> $dT / dx$: temperature gradient
