$$L\{t^nf(t)\} = (-1)^n\frac{d^n}{ds^n}F(s)$$

# Example

$$L\{t\sin kt\}$$
.
$$L\{\sin kt\} = \frac{k}{s^2 + k^2}$$
$$L\{t\sin kt\} = -\frac{d}{ds}\frac{k}{s^2 + k^2} = \frac{2ks}{(s^2 + k^2)^2}$$
