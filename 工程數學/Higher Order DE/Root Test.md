$$\lim_{ m \rightarrow \infty }\sqrt[ m ]{ a_m(x - x_0)^m } < 1$$

$$\implies x - x_0 < \frac{ 1 }{ \lim_{ m \rightarrow \infty }\sqrt[m]{a_m} }$$

$$\implies R < \frac{ 1 }{ \lim_{ m \rightarrow \infty }\sqrt[m]{\vert a_m \vert} }$$
