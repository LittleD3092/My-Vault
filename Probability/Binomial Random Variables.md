A random variable $X$ is Binomial with parameters $(n, p)$ if its [[Probability Mass Function]] is given by:

$$
P(X = k) = 
\left\{
	\begin{array}{l}
		C^n_k p^k (1 - p)^{n - k} & \text{if } k = 0, 1, 2, \dots, n \\
		0 & \text{otherwise}
	\end{array}
\right.
$$
