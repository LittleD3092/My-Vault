Given $n$ distinct objects and have a positive integer $k \leq n$. A $k$-element combination from $n$ objects is defined as:

$$
C^n_k = \frac{n (n - 1)\dots (n - k + 1)}{k!} = \frac{n!}{(n - k)!k!}
$$
