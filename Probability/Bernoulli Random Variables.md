A random variable $X$ with parameter $p$ and only 2 outcomes is called a Bernoulli random variable.

$$
P(X = k) = 
\left\{
	\begin{array}{l}
		p & \text{if }k = 1 \\
		1 - p & \text{if } k = 0 \\
		0 & \text{otherwise}
	\end{array}
\right.
$$
