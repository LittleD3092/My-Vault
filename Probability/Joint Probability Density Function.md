Let $X$ and $Y$ be two continuous random variables. Then, $f_{XY}(x, y)$ is the joint PDF of $X$ and $Y$ if for every subset $B$ of $\mathbb R^2$, we have

$$
P((X, Y) \in B) = \int\int_B f_{XY}(x, y)dxdy
$$
